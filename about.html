Our history

Make up expert is a world-renowned Italian brand specializing in the production of decorative cosmetics and perfumery products. It is currently owned by a large multinational company "Micys Company". Pupa products can be purchased not only in Italy, but also in a number of other European countries, including Russia.

The Make Up Expert brand was founded in 1976 as one of the brands of a large Italian company called Micys Company. Its creators were members of the Gatti family, famous in Italy. Pupa's decorative cosmetics were originally conceived as an exclusively teenage line.

The target audience of the company were young girls aged 12 to 20, as well as

powdery texture, concealers and matting agents (foundations, powders), glosses and lipsticks, some perfumery products.

A kind of innovation, the invention of the company was branded cosmetic sets, which were presented in stores in beautiful holiday packages. This made it possible to purchase products not only for personal use, but also as a gift. In the 1990s, the brand continued to actively develop, offering its customers an ever richer and more extensive line of products.

Currently, Make Up Expert is considered one of the leading cosmetics and perfumery brands in the mass market line.

Our brand philosophy

Experts have developed a unique cosmetics store with the sole purpose of giving joy in the process of recreating the ideal condition of hair. Most women struggle with curls all their lives: damaged, dry, unruly, curly, oily, dull, thin, coarse hair, make it difficult to be confident and achieve success.

The perfect combination of the beneficial properties of tree oils in natural products of the brand helps to get rid of all problems!

Our cosmetics allow every woman to have the hair she has dreamed of all her life. We are inspired to new developments by reviews and letters of gratitude from women who managed to use the brand's products and experienced the simplicity of salon-quality care at home.

The main principle of the brand is the naturalness of each cosmetic development and at the same time the use of high technologies. At the same time, prices for products are available for a wide range of buyers.

The proteins and amino acids contained in the preparations have moisturizing and nourishing qualities. Thanks to their effect, the strands are filled with strength and energy. Correctly selected level of acid-base balance will cope with increased sensitivity of the scalp.

We sell only original cosmetics, all items have quality certificates.
